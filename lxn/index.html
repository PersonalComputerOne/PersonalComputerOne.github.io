Dancing with Shadows: The Unfinished Symphony


    Meaningless under the light
    No history, no laughter in sight
    Walking without a direction
    With each step, the weight of reflection
                
    Darkness wraps around, even though near
    Scenery playing, thoughts unclear
    Origins unheard, sounds unsure
    In silence, noise becomes the cure
                
    In the world where you stand
    Are you happy or just part of the band?
    Used to it, but not really learned
    The swirl of alcohol, always in the right hand
                
    Something changed in the darkness of the norm
    Not the sight but the feelings in the storm
    Returning to the old self
    Quiet, thinking about being oneself
                
    How to act, how to be just right
    Afraid of errors, eyes judging the fight
    Can't bear to look, left with just a glance
    In the shadows, seeking a chance
                
    What have you done?
    Did what's necessary, though not fully won
    Are you happy with the result? Yes,
    But it's clear, it's still not the best
                
    Not everything fell into place
    The voice is incomplete, like the steps' pace
    Happy with hot coffee, a delightful taste
    But alas, it cooled, not the right embrace
                
    The existence won't be erased
    Each moment alone, hoping for a shared space
    Close but distant, the image of a divide
    In a new beginning, where separation does reside
